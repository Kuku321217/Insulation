2 Static Liquid Pressure

P1-OPE = ρE · g · hM · 10−6  •  P1-TEST = ρW · g · ht · 10−6  •  P1-SEIS = ρE · g · hs · 10−6
ρ density (kg/m³), g gravity, h liquid level (mm) → pressure in MPa.

3 Dynamic (Seismic) Liquid Pressure

Pv = √(Pv,c1² + Pv,c2²)  •  P2 = Ph + Pv + Ps  (SRSS of vertical components, then sum of horizontal + vertical + sloshing).

4 Dead Load Compressive Force

w = thickness · density · g · 10−6  •  P3-R = wA+wR+wTA+wL (ring)  •  P3-C = wI+wL1+wTB+wL2 (center)
Seismic: P3-OBE/SSE = (1 + αvr·0.3) · P3-OPE.

5 Line Load Along Inner Shell

ws = W·g / (2πR) / 1000  •  wp = (ρ·9.80665·t/2)·[H − (t/(2μkH))·(1−e−2μkH/t)] / 1000
WL,seis = ws + wp + 0.3·αvf·(ws+wp) + 4M/(πR²·4·106).
